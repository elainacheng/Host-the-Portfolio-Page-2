Pop Mart blind boxes are collectible figures that come in sealed packaging, where you don’t know which specific character you’ll get until you open it.
Each collection normally contains a few thematic, limited vinyl figures that are often created by hot designers or artists.
The mystery of unboxing is the main excitement, where some figures could be rarer or more exclusive than others.
They are popular among collectors who enjoy the thrill of hunting for specific figures to complete their set!
