About Us

Welcome to Sugar & Whiskey, a dessert haven where indulgence meets creativity. We specialize in crafting unique, mouthwatering treats that pair the sweetness of desserts with a dash of boldness from whiskey and other rich flavors. From our signature Bananas Foster to the decadent Porter Bundt Cake with Whiskey-Caramel Sauce, each dish offers a delightful twist. Experience the perfect blend of flavors in every bite, whether it's our tangy Limoncello-Ricotta Cheesecake or the spiced Pavlovas with Mulled Wine Caramel. Sugar & Whiskey is here to elevate your sweet tooth with a touch of elegance and flair.
